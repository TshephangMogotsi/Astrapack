Maintenance & Support

Full SLA Support for Every System We Install

Astra-Pack Holdings offers comprehensive maintenance support and Service Level Agreements for all systems supplied — with a multi-tiered approach to ensure your systems stay fully operational.

Operators trained thoroughly on the effective operation of all installed equipment.

Client maintenance personnel given extensive first-line maintenance training.

Telephone and online support offered as a first response channel for faults.

Technicians available at short notice for on-site faults that cannot be resolved remotely.

Routine preventative maintenance visits and operator retraining included in all SLAs.
